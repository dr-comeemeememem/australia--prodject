    Wombat

    Wombats are short legded marsupials. They are 40 inches long and they have short tails! They can even weigh up to 77 pounds! I found some of them when i was traveling through soulthern Queensland. Realy nice place Mate you should go there... Ok back to the wombat. Wombats are herbivores so that means they eat plants. Wombats' fur can vary from a sandy colour to brown, or from grey to black! They can live up to an astonishing 15 years in the wild! Cool Fact Mate! The Wombat shows a bio-florence when under ultraviolet light.
